About Me

My name is Richard Morgan Shaw and welcome to my portfolio. I specialize in web development, but there’s a lot more to me than that.

I often spend a lot of my free time coding in various different languages for an array of different projects. Most are practical apps such as bots for chat rooms, plugins for browsers or even silly projects like a free cat pic generator.

Sometimes I tackle bigger projects such as video games, which force me to apply a lot of my skills just to put one together. Often I have to deal with putting together data structures, creating a backend system that handles fetching data from the data, which is then thrown into an algorithm that puts all the pieces into place. That’s not even getting into the event system I have to recreate from scratch or the general physics for collision and the various game logic. It even puts my front-end skills by creating a custom UI for each and carefully needing to make sure nothing is obstructive and distracting for the user. As well as my UX skills where I’m constantly streamlining my systems to be less repetitive to save the user time and unnecessary clicks. Gotta make sure I prevent that arthritis!

Beyond my skills as a developer, my family owns two tortoises as pets. A father and son who don’t get along (due to them being territorial animals) and let me tell you both are oddly funny for a couple of slow moving rocks. I also spend my free time doing art appreciation, listening to music, creative writing when the mood strikes me and just generally helping my friends manage any sort of chat room servers they’re in by managing the bots for them.
